What's included in the Shareabouts Monthly Subscription?

With a monthly subscription, we configure Shareabouts for you, using our tried and tested basic template -- you're looking at it right now.

Once you've signed up, we'll work with you to configure you map, including...

  • choosing your basemap - streets (what you see in this version), or satellite.
  • choosing different types of item to collect, with an icon for each
  • setting up your survey form to collect whatever info you want from participants: text, dropdown menus, checkboxes.
  • allow participants to upload photos
  • add your project logo
